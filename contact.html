What to Do When Reporting Bullying

1

Document Everything

Keep records of incidents including dates, times, witnesses, and what happened. Take screenshots if cyberbullying.

2

Tell a Trusted Adult

Speak to a parent, teacher, school counselor, or any adult you trust. Don't keep it to yourself.

3

Report to School Officials

Schools are legally required to address bullying. Report to your teacher, principal, or guidance counselor.

4

Call the Hotlines

If the bullying continues or escalates, call the official hotlines listed above for professional help.
